Tom and Jerry

Tom and Jerry is one of the most beloved animated series, created in 1940 by William Hanna and Joseph Barbera. This iconic cartoon centers around the comedic rivalry between Tom, a house cat, and Jerry, a clever mouse, with their endless chases leading to hilarious moments. Despite their conflicts, Tom and Jerry often find themselves teaming up in tricky situations, showcasing their surprising bond. Their slapstick humor and clever antics have made the show timeless, continuing to entertain generations of fans worldwide, including here in the Philippines, where Tom and Jerry remains a favorite among children and adults alike.
